Exhibitions

Three exhibitions of new fine art works in Scotland

Resipole

A range of new works, including the Kokeshi and Crashland series are unveiled in a 3 week exhibition at Resipole Galleries, near Stephen's studio in Ardnamurchan

St Andrews

Stephen's first major exhibition featuring over 40 works is displayed for the first time at Innis Bookshop Gallery

Dundee

More artwork is put on display at Dundee's Roseangle Arts Gallery. A range of new works, including the full range of Crashland series will be displayed for the first time.
